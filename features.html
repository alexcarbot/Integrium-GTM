In Depth

A closer look at core capabilities

Lead Routing

Every lead to the right person, every time

Traditional round-robin assignment treats all leads equally — but they aren't. A first-time website visitor browsing compact sedans requires a different touch than a returning customer ready to sign on a loaded pickup. Integrium's routing engine analyzes dozens of signals in real time to match each opportunity with the salesperson most likely to convert it.

The system considers product expertise, current workload, historical close rates, and even time-of-day performance patterns. The result is fewer missed connections, faster response times, and more equitable distribution across your sales floor.

  • Multi-signal lead scoring (source, intent, engagement depth, vehicle interest)
  • Dynamic rep matching based on expertise and availability
  • Automatic escalation when SLA thresholds are at risk
  • Full CRM integration — routes appear in your existing workflow
Conversational AI

Conversations that feel human, not scripted

Today's automotive shoppers expect instant, knowledgeable responses at any hour. Integrium's conversational AI doesn't just answer questions — it understands context, remembers previous interactions, and adapts its tone to match your dealership's brand voice.

Whether a customer asks about a specific VIN at midnight or wants to compare trim levels during lunch, the AI responds with accurate, inventory-aware information and can schedule appointments directly. When a conversation requires human expertise, the handoff is seamless — your team gets the full context without the customer repeating a word.

  • Real-time inventory awareness — never quotes a sold vehicle
  • Multi-channel fluency across SMS, web chat, email, and social
  • Appointment scheduling with calendar integration
  • Intelligent human handoff with full conversation context
Compliance

Compliance that's built in, not bolted on

Every automated customer interaction carries regulatory risk. TCPA violations alone can cost $500 to $1,500 per message, and class-action settlements regularly reach eight figures. Integrium was architected with compliance at its core — not added as an afterthought.

The platform maintains granular consent records across every channel, processes opt-out requests in real time (including after-hours), and generates complete audit trails that stand up to regulatory scrutiny. Your legal and compliance teams get the documentation they need without burdening your sales process.

  • Granular consent tracking across all channels and touchpoints
  • Real-time opt-out processing — even at 2 AM
  • Searchable audit logs for every customer interaction
  • TCPA, CASL, and state-level regulation coverage
