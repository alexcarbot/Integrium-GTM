In Depth

A closer look at core capabilities

Clinical Intelligence

AI-enabled rules that think like your best clinicians

Legacy EHRs rely on static alerts that clinicians learn to ignore. Integrium Core's AI-enabled clinical rules engine analyzes the full clinical picture — diagnoses, medications, lab results, care plans, and documentation patterns — to surface actionable insights that actually matter.

The system learns your facility's documentation patterns over time, reducing false positives and prioritizing the gaps most likely to affect resident outcomes or survey results. It is clinical decision support that earns trust instead of alert fatigue.

  • Real-time documentation gap detection across assessments, care plans, and physician orders
  • Medication interaction alerts with clinical severity scoring
  • Care plan consistency checks across interdisciplinary team inputs
  • Adaptive learning that reduces false positives over time
Regulatory Readiness

Your always-on compliance officer

State surveys should not require a two-week scramble. Integrium Core embeds regulatory monitoring into every workflow — CMS Conditions of Participation, state-specific survey requirements, and HIPAA safeguards are enforced continuously, not checked retroactively.

The integrated compliance dashboard gives administrators a real-time view of facility readiness, with drill-down reporting by unit, resident, and regulatory domain. When surveyors arrive, your documentation is already in order because it was built that way from day one.

  • Continuous CMS CoP monitoring with real-time readiness scoring
  • State-specific survey requirement tracking and gap alerts
  • HIPAA access logging and privacy safeguard enforcement
  • Administrator dashboard with drill-down by unit and regulatory domain
Revenue Integrity

Automated billing validation that relieves your providers

Integrium Core automates complex billing validations against Medicare rules directly within the clinical workflow. When a clinician documents a treatment that isn't billable, the system alerts them immediately — not days later when the claim is denied. This is fundamentally different from legacy EHRs that separate clinical documentation from billing compliance.

No existing product combines clinical workflow integration with payer compliance checks the way Integrium Core does. The result: providers make informed decisions at the point of care, denied claims drop dramatically, and your billing team stops spending hours on rework and appeals.

  • Pre-submission PDPM classification validation against clinical record
  • Medicare/Medicaid rule checks with payer-specific edit libraries
  • Clinical-to-billing documentation cross-referencing
  • Denial trend analytics with root-cause identification
