Deterministic Rules, Not ML

Every billing decision is rule-based, producing a full RuleTrace audit trail documenting each step — rule ID, condition, match result, and decision. Defensible billing you can explain to payers.

106 Validated Scenarios

Real-world billing scenarios with dual-reviewer approval workflow and baseline regression checking. The rules engine accuracy is verifiable and testable, not just claimed.
