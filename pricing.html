A rooftop is a single physical dealership location with its own inventory. If you operate multiple brands at one location (e.g., a Honda and a Toyota franchise on the same lot), that typically counts as one rooftop. Multi-franchise stores with separate inventories may count differently — your rep will clarify during scoping.
