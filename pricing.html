Every Plan Includes

No surprises. No hidden costs.

Regardless of which plan you choose, every Integrium Core deployment comes with these fundamentals.

Full HIPAA Compliance

PHI encryption, role-based access controls, audit trails, and breach notification workflows are included at every tier.

Dedicated Onboarding

A named implementation specialist guides your setup from kickoff through go-live. On-site training, data migration, and workflow configuration included.

MDS & Assessment Tools

Streamlined MDS workflows, auto-population from clinical data, and electronic CMS transmission are standard on all plans.

Interoperability

HL7 and FHIR interfaces for connecting with pharmacy systems, labs, billing clearinghouses, and health information exchanges.

Staff Training

Live, role-specific training for nurses, CNAs, MDS coordinators, billing staff, and administrators. On-site and remote sessions included.

Continuous Updates

Regulatory rule updates, AI model improvements, new features, and CMS guideline changes are rolled out at no extra cost.

Implementation fees are waived for annual commitments. For month-to-month agreements, there is a one-time onboarding fee that covers data migration, configuration, and on-site training. Your implementation specialist will provide the exact amount based on your facility’s complexity.
Pricing is based on your facility’s licensed bed count, not current census. This gives you predictable monthly costs regardless of occupancy fluctuations. For multi-facility operators, we calculate across total licensed beds with volume discounts applied.
