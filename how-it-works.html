What to Expect

Your onboarding timeline

From kickoff to full deployment, here's the week-by-week journey to getting Integrium running in your dealership.

Week 1-2

Discovery & Connection

We start with a detailed audit of your current lead flow, tech stack, and operational processes. Then we establish connections with your DMS, CRM, phone system, and communication channels.

  • Technical audit of existing integrations and data flows
  • API connections to your DMS (CDK, Reynolds, Dealertrack, etc.)
  • CRM sync setup with bidirectional data flow
  • Phone system and messaging channel configuration
Week 2-3

Configuration & Training

We configure AI models to understand your specific inventory, pricing, promotions, and brand voice. Your team gets hands-on training with the platform and learns how AI-assisted workflows integrate with their daily routine.

  • AI model customization for your inventory and brand voice
  • Lead scoring rules calibrated to your sales process
  • Routing logic configured for your team structure
  • Staff training sessions (managers and frontline)
Week 3-4

Controlled Launch

We go live with a controlled rollout — starting with after-hours coverage and specific lead sources before expanding to full coverage. Your success team monitors every interaction during this phase.

  • Phased activation starting with after-hours leads
  • Real-time monitoring and quality assurance
  • Daily check-ins with your success manager
  • Rapid iteration based on conversation quality data
Ongoing

Optimization & Growth

Once fully deployed, the platform improves continuously. AI models learn from every interaction, scoring algorithms refine based on conversion data, and your success team provides regular performance reviews with actionable recommendations.

  • Monthly performance reviews with your success team
  • AI model refinement based on real conversion data
  • Quarterly business reviews with leadership
  • Priority access to new features and capabilities
