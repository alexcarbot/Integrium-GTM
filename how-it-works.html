2

Why It Works

Built around how facilities actually operate

No rip-and-replace. No care disruptions. Integrium Core fits into your world from day one.

Zero Downtime Migration

We run parallel systems during transition so clinical staff never lose access to resident records. Your facility operates without interruption throughout the entire migration process.

On-Site Training

Role-specific training for nurses, CNAs, MDS coordinators, billing staff, and administrators. Live, hands-on sessions — not self-service videos — tailored to each team’s daily workflow.

Continuous Improvement

AI models learn from your facility’s clinical data, improving accuracy over time. Quarterly reviews, regulatory updates, and feedback loops ensure the platform evolves with your needs.

What to Expect

Your implementation timeline

From kickoff to full deployment, here’s the week-by-week journey to getting Integrium Core running in your facility.

Week 1–2

Discovery & Assessment

  • Comprehensive facility audit of current workflows and clinical processes
  • Legacy EHR data mapping and migration feasibility analysis
  • Compliance gap assessment against CMS and state survey requirements
  • Stakeholder interviews with nursing, billing, and administrative leadership
Week 3–4

Configuration & Data Migration

  • Clinical rule engine customization for your facility’s care protocols
  • Care plan templates, MDS configuration, and billing workflow setup
  • Historical resident data migration with full validation and reconciliation
  • Integration connections with pharmacy, lab, and billing clearinghouse systems
Week 5–6

Training & Parallel Run

  • Role-specific on-site training for nurses, CNAs, MDS coordinators, and billing staff
  • Parallel running with legacy system to validate data accuracy and workflows
  • Real-time monitoring and daily check-ins with your implementation team
  • Rapid iteration based on staff feedback and documentation quality data
Week 7+

Go-Live & Optimization

  • Phased go-live starting with one unit before facility-wide rollout
  • On-site support team present during the critical first weeks of operation
  • Continuous AI model refinement based on real clinical documentation data
  • Quarterly business reviews with facility leadership and ongoing regulatory updates
